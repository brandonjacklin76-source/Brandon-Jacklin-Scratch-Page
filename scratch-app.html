Nation Builder

Buy, defend, and grow your trade empire.

How to Play
  • Trade: Buy goods when prices are low, sell when high. Watch prices fluctuate each turn.
  • Build Cities: Click on green plains on the map to build cities (costs 5 grain, 2 textiles, 1 spice).
  • Grow Cities: Click on cities to upgrade them (increasing costs). Higher levels produce more income.
  • Manage Population: Cities need grain to grow population. Population produces income but consumes food.
  • Hire Knights: Buy knights for defense. Each knight reduces attack damage by 5. With 20+ knights, attacks are completely repelled.
  • Events: Every 10 turns: economic depression (halve prices if <3 cities). Every 15 turns: enemy attack (damage reduced by cities + knights).
  • Goal: Build a thriving empire! Balance trade, resources, city management, and defense.
